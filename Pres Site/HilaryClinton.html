Hilary D. Clinton

Hillary Clinton was elected to the U.S. Senate in 2001 and became the first American first lady to ever win a public office seat. She also became the 67th U.S. secretary of state in 2009 to 2013. Clinton became the first woman in U.S. history to become the presidential nominee of a major political party in 2016. She was born on October 26, 1947, in Chicago, Illinois, where she earned her law degree from Yale University and later married her fellow law school graduate Bill Clinton in 1975.
